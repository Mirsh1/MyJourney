About US

The general store is a type of shop placed in many locations in RuneScape. Unlike specialty shops, general stores usually accept all tradeable items, making them a useful place to sell miscellaneous goods that do not have a dedicated store. General stores are identified on the map by a pot General store icon.png icon. Many general stores have both a shop keeper and a shop assistant, both of which can be used to access the shop stock.
Dragon Scimitar

The dragon scimitar is the strongest scimitar available in Old School RuneScape. It can only be wielded by players who have 60 Attack and have completed the Monkey Madness I quest. It also shares similar bonuses with Viggora's chainmace, with the chainmace being a crush weapon rather than a slash weapon.
